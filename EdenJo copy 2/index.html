Wish is a slim, narrow serif typeface designed to bring subtle elegance and refined charm to your work.

Its delicate and modern aesthetic makes it versatile for a variety of creative applications.

Perfect for Posters and Book Covers. Wish commands attention with its striking presence while maintaining a soft, understated appeal.

Whether it’s a captivating poster, a poetic book cover, or refined invitations, Wish elevates any design with its unique character.

It’s more than a typeface, it is a tool to convey depth and meaning through every letter.
